Refund and cancellation policy

Refunds

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Fixed-scope services

If work has not started, a fixed-scope service can be cancelled for a full refund. Once work has started, refunds are evaluated based on completed work and unused scope.

Digital deliverables

For downloadable or delivered digital products, refund requests are reviewed within 7 days of purchase. Refunds may be declined after substantial use, download, or delivery.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions can be cancelled before the next billing period. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically refund prior periods already delivered.

Disputes

Contact support first so the issue can be reviewed with the project scope, payment record, and delivered artifacts.
